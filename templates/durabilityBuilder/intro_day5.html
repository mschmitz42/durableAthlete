WAIT!! Don’t forget today’s Mobility & Technique Challenge:

Most triathletes ignore ankles—but locked-up ankles kill power, speed and create movement compensations all the way up the chain. Ankles with full, native range of motion means more speed and stability on the run, bike and believe it or not, even the swim. The high-cadence Isolated Leg Training (ILT) drill smooths out your pedal stroke, helping you ride faster with less effort.

  • Daily Mobility: Ankle dorsiflexion band optional
  • Technique Drill: Bike-(ILT)Isolated Leg Training
