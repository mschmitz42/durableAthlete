What is the Durability Builder?

Conversations in endurance sports, more often than not, circle back to the same thing: stiffness, strains, and injuries. I’ve been there, and chances are, you have too.

That’s why I built the Durability Builder.

It’s an online multisport training platform designed to take triathletes from injury-prone and frustrated to durable, confident triathletes, through technique, mobility, and smart training that lasts.

It’s not about stacking endless miles. It’s about pairing your swim, bike, and run with the work that keeps you moving well, recovering faster, and training consistently.

That’s how you stop breaking down and start breaking through.

And you won’t be doing it alone. When you start, you’ll also get access to the Durable Triathlete community.

So welcome!
