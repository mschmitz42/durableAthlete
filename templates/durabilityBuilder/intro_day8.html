My biggest regret (don’t make the same mistake)

As a triathlete and a coach, I’ve been where you are. Battling those relentless overuse injuries and sifting through heaps of conflicting information was a journey in itself. And I can tell you – nothing’s more frustrating than going in circles.

You know the routine: try this advice, then that one. But nothing seemed to work quite right or cover all the bases. I eventually realized, if I was going to get out of pain and back to peak performance, I needed a deeper understanding of the human body and what truly works for athletes like us.

So, I did just that. I went back to school, dug into human physiology, earned certifications, and devoted years to building a better approach. But even after all that, I still hadn’t found a program that checked all the boxes.

That’s why I created one myself.

My Durability Builder program isn’t just a random collection of exercises – it’s the result of years of study, hard lessons, and a whole lot of time spent figuring out exactly what gets real results for triathletes. And it’s specifically for athletes like you, who want to get out of pain, stay strong, and go faster.

Imagine having a program that’s built with your exact needs in mind, tailored to prevent injuries, and improve performance in a way that actually lasts. You can skip the endless trial-and-error and start making measurable progress, right from Day 1.

There are two ways to pay: time or money. I learned this the hard way. If I could change one thing, it would be investing in the right solution sooner. The question isn’t just how to reach your goals—it’s who will guide you. I’ve spent years uncovering the answers, and now, I’m here to show you exactly how to get there, faster, healthier, and without the guesswork.

Years ago, I made the choice to commit fully to my own training, and it transformed everything. Now, I’m offering that same support to you. Because the quickest path back to your best self is having the right guide at your side.
